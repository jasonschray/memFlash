Social

We consider learning to be a social activity. That is why our users can create descriptive profiles with custom profile images and interact with other users in a forum setting. In the future we plan to implement friends lists and peer-to-peer messages.

Flashcards

Our smart flashcards are built to allow our users to learn many different kinds of relationships. Our cards support images, audio files, and multiple writing systems (including Chinese, Japanese, and Mathematical Symbols).

Self-Testing

The core philosophy of our site is that people should use this app to teach themselves. We want our users to use our systems for self betterment. As a result, there is no way for users to administer testing. Our tests track users proficiency in a given flashcard and adjust how testing is done to reduce time-to-memorize.
